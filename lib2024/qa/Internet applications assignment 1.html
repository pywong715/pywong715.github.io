Critic's pick: Yiyi

Synopsis

    YiYi, an awarding winning Taiwan movie, depicts the moral dilemmas and everyday struggles in a typical middle-class Taipei family.

    Through the camera held by Yang Yang, the youngest child of the family, audiences will witness and observe
    his family members' "ups and downs" in life.

    This three-hour movie brings the audience on a life-long journey; experiencing regrets and loves in childhood,
    adolescence, middle-age and the senior years.
